Purpose

Growing up I've seen lots of Maori and Pasifika families struggle with finances. Maori and Pasifika people are more vulnerable to financial issues and this is largely due to the lack of resources, discussions and guidance around topics like managing debt, having multiple streams of outcome, investing etc.

So I created this space to educate and provide resources for our Maori and Pasifika youth so that we can thrive and not let money dictate our lives but rather give us options in life. This educational resource has been created to help Māori and Pasifika students learn about financial literacy. The aim is to educate and empower Māori and Pasifika youth to embark on their journey towards financial literacy.

Designed by our people, for our people. As I continue to develop this website I strive to incorporate as much flexibility as possible because I understand not everyone has the same learning needs and this should not be a barrier.

Disclaimer I am not a financial advisor this is just general advice about financial literacy for educational purposes
