Introduction to Financial Literacy: Money Mindsets

Financial literacy. Although this may sound big and scary, do not stress Money Modules is here to break down these confusing concepts

First of all lets discuss money mindsets. Our money mindset is our values and beliefs that influence how we choose to manage our money.

Understanding our money mindsets will help us to identify what really matters to us and our potential weaknesses where we could develop better money habits. Financial indepence will be the end goal for most of us, where money give us options in life. For example the option to buy our own home, the option to invest in our education, the option to help out our families while still being able to provide for our own etc.

It's important to remeber that financial indpendence is a long term journey. So when setting our financial goals the aim isn't to get rid of all the stuff we enjoy. For example we may value going out with friends, in the long term getting rid of this isn't very sustainable instead we may try to limit outings to once a week instead of 3-4 times a week
